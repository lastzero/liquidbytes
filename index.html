Liquid Bytes Technologies was founded in 2001 by Michael Mayer. Since inception, it has a proven track record of successfully completed software projects.

In 2006 Michael decided not to continue Liquid Bytes as a full service provider, but to follow his passion and focus on his outstanding skills as an engineer and consultant for PHP, JavaScript and SQL.

You can hire him either as a short-term consultant or freelance developer for your own project.
